## Brand System

### Colour Palette
| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Forest Green | `#1B4D3E` | Logo, headings, primary backgrounds |
| Secondary | Digital Emerald | `#2E8B57` | CTAs, growth indicators |
| Accent | Tech Mint | `#A7FFEB` | Data touchpoints, hover states |
| Neutral | Mint White | `#F1F8F6` | Page backgrounds |
| Text (dark bg) | Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | Body text on dark |
| Text (light bg) | Neutral Grey | `#333333` | Body copy on light |
| Store primary | Retail Green | `#2AA84E` | Store section only |
| Store accent | Warm Orange | `#ED7D28` | Store section only |

### Typography
| Use | Font | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Headlines | Montserrat | Bold / ExtraBold |
| Body text | Inter | Regular / Medium |

### Logo Rules
- Master V-icon only on corporate site
- Forest Green on white · White/Tech Mint on dark
- No drop shadows, no 3D effects
- Safe space = height of "V" character
- ❌ No orange on corporate canvas
- ❌ No gold, no deep blue

### Visual Language
- Thin lines + circular nodes → data flow and connectivity
- Negative space used intentionally
- Grid-based layouts → precision and corporate authority

### Design Directives
- ✅ Monochromatic green for all corporate and Solutions touchpoints
- ✅ Tech Mint for digital/data accents only
- ✅ Orange permitted in Store section only
- ✅ Mobile-first layout
- ✅ Vietnamese diacritics-safe fonts (Montserrat + Inter)

## Page 2 — About ✅

### Section 1 — Company Story

> **VSON began with a question two founders kept asking themselves.**
>
> After years at FPT — one of Vietnam's largest technology and distribution corporations — Frederick Dang and Henry Hung had seen the market from the inside. They knew how products moved, where the gaps were, and why so many great brands never fully reached the consumers who needed them. So they left to build something different.
>
> The first chapter was about trust. VSON started by bringing world-class consumer brands — carefully selected, rigorously sourced — into Vietnamese homes. Building that retail presence took patience, deep market knowledge, and a relentless focus on quality. Over time, it created something more valuable than a product catalogue: a distribution network, a loyal customer base, and a reputation that opened doors.
>
> Those open doors led to a second chapter. Brands who trusted VSON to distribute their products began asking for more — how to activate campaigns, how to reach new audiences, how to grow. VSON answered with solutions: merchandise, activation campaigns, and the commercial intelligence to make them work. The B2B business grew from the same roots as the retail business — real market experience, not theory.
>
> The next chapter is already underway. VSON is becoming the partner of choice for international companies — particularly those from Singapore — who see Vietnam's opportunity but need someone who truly knows the terrain. That confidence comes from the combined strengths of the founding team: deep operational experience, commercial instincts built across hundreds of brand partnerships, and the cross-border perspective needed to turn market ambition into market presence.
>
> The name has always carried the vision. **VSON — Việt Sing** — is a bridge. Between Vietnam and Singapore. Between world-class brands and the consumers who deserve them. Between ambition and execution.
