## Common Questions While Reviewing

**Q: I don't understand the 4 pillars concept. Should I?**

A: No. Just signal: "approve as written" or flag specific issues that matter to you.

**Q: What if I disagree with the design?**
A: Flag it now. This is the right time to change. Design → Code → Launch. Now is cheapest.

**Q: Can I modify the documents?**
A: No, but you can comment and suggest changes in your reply to Fred.

**Q: How long does this actually take?**
A: 10 minutes (quick scan) to 45 minutes (deep dive). You choose your level.
