## Website Delivery Pod (CX/MarTech Overlay)

**Category:** CX and Digital Experience
**Cynefin Domain:** Complicated (Expert)
**JTBD:** Accelerate your CX/MarTech team's website delivery with structured governance, quality automation, and reusable technical accelerators, so your developers ship performant, accessible, standards-compliant sites on schedule.

**COI:** $50K-210K per project in rework, duplication, delayed launch, and compliance gaps.

### SC Note

Recommend this Pod when: enterprise client has an existing CX/MarTech developer team but lacks governance, quality standards, or delivery methodology for website projects. Vinova overlays structure onto their team rather than replacing it. Entry point is the Pilot tier (architecture review + QC audit) to demonstrate value before committing to a full engagement.

Do NOT recommend when: client has no developers (they need a full-build Pod, not an overlay) or when the site requires a CMS/framework build (CMS integration pattern not yet in Standard Core — expected after Pod 2).
