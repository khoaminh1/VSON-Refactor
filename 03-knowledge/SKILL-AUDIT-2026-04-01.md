# VSON Corporate Website vs VS- Skills — Audit & Skill Update Recommendations

**Date:** 2026-04-01
**Author:** Claude (audit) — requires Fred review
**Purpose:** The corporate website represents Fred's most current thinking about VSON's business. This audit identifies where the existing VS- skills are now outdated and recommends specific updates.

---

## Executive Summary

The corporate website has evolved VSON's positioning significantly beyond what the skills describe. The biggest shifts are: a new brand architecture (VSON Store / VSON Solutions replacing An Gia / An Phú publicly), a restructured B2B offering (3-layer Activation Solutions + MRA-backed Market Development replacing the 4 offering types), the addition of the "Phygital ecosystem" identity, a new leadership structure, and Singapore as a strategic market (not just HQ for Advisory). The skills need to catch up.

---

## 1. Brand Architecture — Major Shift

### What the skills say (vs-core, vs-b2b-service-design)
- Legal name: VIET SING TECHNOLOGY INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED
- Internal brands: An Gia (B2C), An Phú (B2B)
- B2C: Lead with product brands (Torayvino, etc.), VSON as endorser
- B2B: Lead with VSON corporate brand
- No explicit parent/child brand hierarchy

### What the website says
- Public-facing brand architecture:
  ```
  VSON (Master / Parent Brand)
  ├── VSON Store (B2C Retail — internal: An Gia)
  └── VSON Solutions (B2B Solutions — internal: An Phú)
  ```
- "An Gia" and "An Phú" are internal names only — English-facing materials use VSON Store / VSON Solutions exclusively
- VSON is positioned as a "Phygital Commerce Ecosystem" — not just a distributor
- Master V-icon logo for corporate; separate visual identity for Store (orange accents)

### Skill updates needed

| Skill | Update |
|-------|--------|
| **vs-core** | Add formal brand architecture (VSON → VSON Store + VSON Solutions). Add rule: An Gia / An Phú = internal Vietnamese only, never on English-facing materials. Add "Phygital Commerce Ecosystem" as approved positioning language. |
| **vs-b2b-service-design** | Replace generic "VSON B2B" framing with "VSON Solutions" as the public-facing B2B brand. |
| **vs-b2c-marketing** | Replace generic "VSON B2C" references with "VSON Store" as the public-facing B2C brand. |
| **vs-b2c-listing** | Same — use "VSON Store" consistently. |

## 2. B2B Service Lines — Restructured

### What the skills say (vs-b2b-service-design)
4 offering types:
1. OT-1: Corporate Gifting & Brand Activation
2. OT-2: Omni-channel Distribution Services
3. OT-3: CX Consulting with PangoCDP
4. OT-4: B2C Retail (DTC eCommerce)

### What the website says
2 solution tracks under VSON Solutions:

**Track 1 — Activation Solutions** (for Brands)
- 3-layer capability stack:
  - Layer 1: Merchandise & Activation (physical)
  - Layer 2: Data & Campaign Platform (intelligence)
  - Layer 3: Marketing & Automation (scale)
- This consolidates old OT-1 + OT-3 + parts of OT-2 into a single integrated offering
- PangoCDP is no longer called out by name — it's embedded in "Data & Campaign Platform"
- "Campaign Intelligence" and "Marketing Automation" are new capability labels

**Track 2 — Market Development** (for Singapore Corporates)
- Entirely new service track not in any skill
- Tied to Enterprise Singapore MRA Grant (up to S$100,000, 70% funded)
- 3 services mapping to MRA pillars:
  1. Overseas Business Development (partner search, BD strategy)
  2. Overseas Market Set-up (entity formation, compliance)
  3. Overseas Market Promotion (brand visibility, PR)
- Target: Singapore SMEs entering Vietnam

### Skill updates needed

| Skill | Update |
|-------|--------|
| **vs-b2b-service-design** | Major rewrite. Replace 4 OT types with 2 solution tracks (Activation Solutions + Market Development). Update Service Card templates accordingly. Move OT-4 (B2C Retail) out of B2B entirely — it belongs under VSON Store. |
| **vs-b2b-presales** | Update qualification framework to reflect 2 tracks instead of 4 OTs. Add MRA grant knowledge for Market Development deals (eligibility criteria, grant caps, 70% funding rate, 3 pillars). |
| **vs-strategy-business-plan** | Update growth vectors — Market Development via MRA is a new vector not currently captured. CX consulting is no longer a standalone offering but embedded in Activation Solutions Layer 2. |
| **vs-b2b-contracting** | May need MRA-specific contract templates (grant-related compliance, Enterprise Singapore reporting requirements). |
